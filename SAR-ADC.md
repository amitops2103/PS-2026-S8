
# 8 BIT SAR-ADC
------
The main fundamental building blocks of a SAR-ADC are :         
        |___ **1. Sample & Hold circuit**      
        |___ **2. DAC**     
        |___ **3. Comparator**     
        |___ **4. SAR logic (Successive Aproximation register)**
